Rohit Sharma

Captain • Opener • Hitman of Indian Cricket

About Him

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer and current captain of the Indian team. Known for his stylish batting and powerful sixes, he's the only player with three ODI double centuries and the highest ODI score—264 runs.

Achievements

  • Captain of India and Mumbai Indians
  • 3 Double Centuries in ODIs
  • 264 – Highest ODI Score
  • Known for calm and cool leadership
